              How to play

              Add players (or join a room), then draw cards. On your turn: read the card aloud and either do it / answer honestly, or take the Drink / Pass option. Then it's the next player.

              The levels

              • L1 Icebreaker — light, funny warm-ups.
              • L2 Spicy — flirty and bold, still tame.
              • L3 Freaky — adults only, consent-first dares.
              • L4 OMG — high chaos. Adults who clearly opted in.

              Modes

              Solo / Pass the Phone — one device, pass it around the circle.

              Multiplayer — one person creates a room; everyone else joins with the code and every phone stays in sync.

              Make it yours

              Add your own custom cards in setup (or in the room, if you're the host), pick which levels are in play, and use the safety toggles to keep things comfortable.

              Safety first

              Consent always wins. You can pass or skip anything — no one is ever forced. Keep L3/L4 to adults who opted in, and please drink responsibly (the game works just as well with a soft drink).
